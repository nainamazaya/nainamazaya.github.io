Projects

Study Journaling for Physics

The reason I did this project is because I like physics but have a little difficulty understanding it. I want to deepen my independent learning skills in physics lessons and help my friends by making summaries that can be accessed on social media.
First of all, I study the material from videos on several learning platforms (applications, YouTube, textbooks), then I will make rough notes. After I succeeded in understanding along with practice questions, I started to summarize the rough notes into a neat summary that was easy to read. Finally, I uploaded the summary to my project Instagram.

Data Analysis for Kardio Health

My second project is related to heart and lung health. I chose this project because I like sports and want to build a regular exercise habit. I also want to hone my interest in processing data.
The activities I do are cycling, swimming and walking. I cycle twice a week and swim once a week. In the third month, I alternated with walking activities. While exercising, I measure my heart rate and maximum oxygen levels in my lungs using a digital watch. After that, I input it into a table and made it into a graph so I could see the progress.

Data Analysis for Different Kefir Treatments

My third project is related to my second project, namely healthy living. In this project I want to make a probiotic drink resulting from bacterial fermentation. I make kefir with varying fermentation times. Then I measured and recorded the pH of the kefir which was influenced by the length of fermentation time and room temperature.
from that, I also make a simple kefir mask. I posted the results of the data on Instagram along with several facts about kefir.
In this project I learned to patiently wait for the results. This project exercised my curiosity about several different treatments.
